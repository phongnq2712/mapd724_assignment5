Toronto City Introduction for new Visitors

 

Toronto Geographic coordinates

 

43°42′N, 79°24′W

 

Toronto Airport Name

 

Toronto Pearson International Airport (IATA: YYZ, ICAO: CYYZ)
Toronto Airport to City

Toronto Pearson International airport is located 22.5 km or 14.0 mi northwest of Downtown Toronto.

 

Toronto Information

 

Toronto is a biggest and prominent Canadian city along Lake Ontario’s northwestern shore in North America.

Toronto is the capital of province of Ontario in Canada.

Toronto is the largest city of Canada and known for its skyscrapers and high rise buildings.

Toronto is also called as business capital of Canada as it is home to headquarters to all major large corporations and banks in Canada.

 

Toronto Area (Metro)

 

2,280.21 sq m

 

Toronto Population (Metro)

 

5,928,040

 

Language(s) in Toronto

 

English and French are official languages
